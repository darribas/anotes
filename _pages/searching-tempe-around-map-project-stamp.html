Searching Tempe Around Map-Project (S.T.A.M.P.)

This is a personal project which tries to collect coffee houses, bars, restaurants and every kind of business with a little bit more charm than any commercial chain spread everywhere. I'll be happy if I get to know this is helping other people feeling living in a more human space as well and, of course, I'll be glad to add all the suggestions you do so feel free to drop a comment I'll greatly appreciate it. Thank you very much in advance.

"Because diversity and uniqueness speaks of human people in human places, not of machines in empty spaces"
